ZeroChat

Zero JavaScript in your browser.

Zero accounts, cookies, or logs.

No tracking, fingerprinting, or data storage.


Send files to your friends (or yourself) by having your browsers go to the same room name, such as /secret-room. When you join with a passcode, a unique string of characters shows up to identify you next to your name. All data about a message is securely deleted from memory after each message has been sent.
